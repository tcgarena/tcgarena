RULES:

Tournament will be played under the exact same rules as a COMP-REL event with a few alterations listed below:

  • Players who create an irreversible gamestate will receive a game loss. This includes, but is not limited to:
        1. Causing yourself to mulligan in the middle of a game.
        2. Dragging an “unknown” card to your deck and shuffling.
  • Disconnections from a game are given a 5 minute total “forgiveness”. This means that players will have 5 total minutes per match to be disconnected before receiving a game-loss.
  • Players who are late to their match past 5 minutes will receive a game loss, and players who are late to their match past 10 minutes will receive a match loss and be removed from the tournament.
